Medas Clinic Tele Medicine App (Android & iOS)

Medas Clinic Tele Medicine App is designed to provide premium health care and support to users on the go from their mobile devices. The App includes features like Appointment Bookings & Consultations, manage Lab Reports, Vitals, Case Sheets, Prescriptions and much more. The App is developed for both Android & iOS

  • Rating:
  • Client: Medas Clinic
  • Platforms: Android & iOS
  • Technologies Used: Ionic Framework, Rest API & Firebase
  • Completed: 16 June 2020

★ Appointment Bookings

★ Consultation

★ View all Upcoming and Previous Appointments

★ Payment

★ View & Manage Case Sheet

★ Manage, View and Download Lab Reports

★ View & Manage Vitals

★ Manage Assigned Specialists & Doctors
