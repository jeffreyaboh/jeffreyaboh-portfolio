Kafas Xpress Gas (Android & iOS Gas Ordering App)

Kafas Xpress Gas Ordering Mobile Application for both Android and iOS allows you to order gas cylinders, gas refills and request other products and services from the comfort of your home on your mobile device and get it delivered to you.

  • Rating:
  • Client: Kafas Xpress Gas
  • Website: www.kafasxpressgas.com
  • Platforms: Android, iOS & Web
  • Technologies Used: Ionic Framework & Firebase
  • Completed: 21 February 2020
