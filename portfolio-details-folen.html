Folen Grain Credit Scheme (Android Data Capturing App)

Folen Grain Credit Scheme Data Capturing App is designed to capture bio-data of farmers, sellers in the grains business and help provide them with instant loans and credits for a period of time. It is typically used by registered agents. Developed for Android only.

  • Rating:
  • Client: Folen Grain Credit Scheme
  • Website: www.folen.ng
  • Platforms: Android
  • Technologies Used: Ionic Framework & Firebase
  • Completed: 29 April 2020

★ Geolocation mapping
